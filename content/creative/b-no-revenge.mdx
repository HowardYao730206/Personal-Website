## About this cover

I worked on this cover before I had learned how Japanese kana and pronunciation actually worked. And thus most of the syllables and accents in this song were recreated entirely by me hearing and imitating, with a lot of trial involved of course!

I tried to import this MIDI and phoneme setup into other vocal synthesis engines (Vocaloid 6 & Ace Studio) and it produced some questionable results.

I guess a reminder that I should study harder on Japanese. Haha. (*^_^*)

## Background

This cover features Rose and Kasane Teto on Synthesizer V (SV). Unlike traditional vocal synthesis engines like Vocaloid, Synthesizer V uses modern AI-based synthesis technology to generate more natural vocal expression — many of them are so-called AI voicebanks.
