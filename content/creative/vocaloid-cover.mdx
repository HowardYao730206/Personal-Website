## What is Vocaloid?

Vocaloid is a singing voice synthesis software that allows creators to produce vocals using virtual voicebanks (Hatsune Miku for this case). Instead of recording a human singer, melodies and lyrics are entered into software and synthesized into a vocal performance.

## What is Vocal Tuning?

Vocal tuning is the process of creating and shaping a synthesized vocal performance. My work includes writing and editing melodies, entering lyrics, refining phonemes and pronunciation, adjusting timing, pitch, dynamics, and vocal expression to create a performance that fits the emotion of the songs.
