---
title: "On making things that no one might see"
date: "2026-05-10"
tags: ["creativity", "music"]
excerpt: "Sometimes the act of creating is entirely for yourself."
---

Sometimes I finish a Vocaloid arrangement at 2am and think: no one is going to hear this.

And yet I keep making them.

Making something forces you to have an opinion. When you arrange a song, you have to decide what it's *about*. That practice of opinion-forming turns out to be useful everywhere else.

So I keep making things. Even the ones no one sees.
